What We Stand For

The Blue Diamond Standard

Seven criteria. One standard. A neighborhood that earns the name Blue Diamond.

01

Intergenerational Living by Design

Housing that serves all ages — not in separation, but in proximity. Children thrive alongside elders. Every generation is enriched.

02

Housing for Every Life Stage

From rental apartments to ownership cottages and luxury residences — a full lifecycle of living options within one community.

03

Integrated Senior Services

Trusted care partners within the community. Residents never need to leave the neighborhood they love as their needs change.

04

A Culture of Active Living

Movement, engagement, and connection woven into daily life — trails, courts, gardens, and gathering spaces that invite participation.

05

Vibrant Mixed-Use Integration

Childcare, dining, healthcare, fitness, salon, and more — all within walking distance, supporting an 80% walkable lifestyle.

06

Exceptional Land Quality

Quality soil, stable terrain, and thoughtful water management — a physical foundation designed for beauty that endures.

07

Forward-Thinking Municipal Partnership

Collaboration with municipalities that share our vision — TIF districts, infrastructure coordination, and streamlined development. Not transactional. Strategic and future-focused.

Now Developing — Our First Blue Diamond Community

Cherry Creek

Blue Diamond Communities' inaugural development — where the standard comes to life for the first time. Cherry Creek isn't just a neighborhood. It's proof that a better way of living together is possible.

Mission

A Neighborhood Designed So You Never Want to Leave

Cherry Creek was built around a single question: what does it look like when a community truly works for everyone? The answer is a walkable, intergenerational neighborhood where healthy living, meaningful relationships, and everyday convenience come together — naturally, beautifully, and for every age.

From young families putting down roots to retirees choosing vitality over isolation — Cherry Creek is a place where wisdom is shared, friendships are formed, and everyone belongs.

Seven Visions for Cherry Creek

1

Wellness in Everyday Living

Walkable streets, trails, green spaces, and fresh-food access that make healthy choices the natural ones.

2

Intergenerational Connection

Shared spaces and programming that bring generations together — mentorship, clubs, and daily life lived side by side.

3

80% Walkable Lifestyle

Retail, dining, healthcare, and services integrated on-site — connected through safe, beautiful paths.

4

A Culture of Care & Belonging

Neighbor-to-neighbor relationships, community events, and a culture built on kindness and shared responsibility.

5

Thoughtful Design & Lasting Quality

Timeless architecture, quality materials, and public spaces maintained to the highest standard.

6

Meaningful & Convenient Living

Spaces that support hobbies, learning, and creativity — so residents can focus on what matters most.

7

Purpose-Driven Community Life

Lifelong learning, volunteerism, and personal growth — a life lived with intention and joy.

Cherry Creek & Greencroft

Two mission-aligned organizations united by a shared belief: that people deserve to stay in the community they love — at every stage of life.

About Greencroft

Compassionate Care, Rooted in Community

Greencroft is a well-established nonprofit senior living organization offering a full continuum of care — from independent living to memory care and rehabilitation. Their values-driven, person-first approach makes them the ideal partner for Cherry Creek.

  • Full continuum — independent living through skilled nursing
  • Values-driven, person-first approach
  • Strong track record and community reputation
  • Nonprofit, mission-centered ethos
  • Assisted living, memory care & rehabilitation
